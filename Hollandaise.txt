HOLLANDAISE


Ingredients
===========
3 Egg Yolks
1/4 teaspoon Dijon Mustard
1 Tablespoon Lemon juice
3 Dashes Tobasco
1/2 cup Butter, melted

Method
======
1. Separate yolks into blender.
2. Add Dijon Mustard, Lemon Juice, Tobasco.
3. Blend until all mixed.
4. Blend on Low, and drizzle with melted Butter to emulsify.
5. Once all Butter is added, close top, and turn on High.
6. Pour to serve.
