LARB CHICKEN LETTUCE WRAPS

Ingredients
-----------
3/4 cup canned low-salt chicken broth
1 1/2 pounds ground chicken

1 cup thinly sliced green onions
3/4 cup thinly sliced shallots
3 tablespoons minced fresh lemongrass*
1 tablespoon thinly sliced Thai chilies* or serrano chilies

1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro leaves
1/3 cup chopped fresh mint leaves

Sauce

2/3 cup fresh lime juice
1/3 cup fish sauce (nam pla*)
1 tablespoon sugar
2 teaspoons Thai roasted chili paste* in oil or chili-garlic sauce*

2 small heads Boston lettuce, separated into leaves

METHOD
------
0. Start browning chicken in pan.
1. Stir together Sauce ingredients and set aside. 
2. Once half the chicken is browned, add Green Onions, Shallots, Lemongrass and Chilies.
3. Remove from heat when veggies are tender, all chicken is browned, and liquid is reduced. 
4. Stir in the Cilantro, Mint and Sauce.
5. Spoon mixture into Bib Lettuce leaves.
