MAKE-AHEAD GRAVY
(New York Times November 14, 2007)
Reprinted again in 2018.

Ingredients
-----------
1 stick Butter
1/2 c Onion, minced
1/2 c Flour
5 c Stock, homemade, warmed

Salt & Pepper

Method
------
1. Melt Butter in large saucepan. 
2. Add Onion; cook until translucent.
3. Sprinkle Flour, stirring constantly, until Flour is golden brown. 
4. Whisk-in stock until smooth.
5. Cool, cover, chill for max five (5) days.
