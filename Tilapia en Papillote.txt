TILAPIA EN PAPILLOTE
Matchstick veggies: 
Shallot
Fennel
Carrot
Peppers
Zucchini

Seasoning: 
1 Tbsp Lemon juice
1/4 tsp Thyme, whole leaves
3-5 grinds Black Pepper

Whitefish: 
Tilapia
Mahi Mahi

Place fish on large square of parchment.
Cover with Shallot & Fennel.
Cover with remaining Veggies.
Sprinkle with seasoning.
