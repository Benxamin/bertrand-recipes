INGREDIENTS
-----------
Crust

3 tablespoons Butter, softened
1/2 cup Sugar
3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
3/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/3 cup finely chopped walnuts

Filling

1 package (8 ounces) reduced-fat cream cheese
1/4 cup plus 1 tablespoon sugar, divided
1 large egg, room temperature
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 can (15 ounces) reduced-sugar sliced pears, drained well and thinly sliced
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon


METHOD
------
0. Preheat oven to 425˚F.
1. Beat Butter, Sugar and Cinnamon until crumbly.
2. Beat in Flour and Walnuts.
3. Press into bottom and sides of a 9-inch fluted tart pan with removable bottom coated with cooking spray.
4. Beat Cream Cheese and 1/4 cup Sugar until smooth. 
5. Beat in Egg and Vanilla.
6. Spread into crust.
7. Arrange pears over top. 
8. Mix cinnamon and remaining sugar; sprinkle over pears.
9. Bake 10 minutes.
10. Reduce oven setting to 350˚F.
11. Bake until filling is set, 15-20 minutes.
12. Cool for one hour on a wire rack. 
12. Refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
