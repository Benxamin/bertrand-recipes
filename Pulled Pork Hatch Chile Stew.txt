PULLED PORK HATCH CHILE STEW


Ingredients
-----------
1 Tbsp Olive Oil
1/2 White Onion, diced
1 cup Carrot, diced
6 Hatch Chile Peppers, halved, seeded, roasted, peeled
3 cloves Garlic, diced
1 Tbsp Black Pepper, ground

1 1/2 pounds shredded cooked pork
~~1/4 cup all-purpose flour~~
4 cups chicken broth
1 (15 ounce) can Tomatoes, diced (with juice)
1 Potatoe, large Russet, cubed
1/2 teaspoon Cumin, ground


Method
------
0. Heat Oil in a large pot over medium heat (5 of 10).
1. Add White Onion. Cook and stir until starting to turn translucent. About 10 minutes.
2. Add Carrots and cook until almost soft, about 10 minutes.
3. Add Garlic and Chile Peppers; cook and stir until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
4. Stir in pork, season with Black Ground Pepper.
5. Stir chicken broth slowly into the pot.
6. Add Tomatoes and juice, Potatoes, and Cumin.
7. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and cover.
8. Simmer, stirring occasionally, until potatoes are tender, 25 minutes.
