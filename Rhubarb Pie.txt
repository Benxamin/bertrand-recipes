RHUBARB PIE

Ingredients
-----------
6 stalks Rhubarb, sliced
1 Lime, juiced
1/2 cup white granulated sugar
1/4 cup Turbinado sugar
1 tsp Thyme
1 tsp Allspice
1 tsp Nutmeg
1 tsp Salt
2 Ready-made pie crusts (in your grocerâ€™s refrigerated section)


Method
------

0. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
1. Slice 6 big stalks of Rhubarb into a large metal bowl.
2. Squeeze in the juice of one Lime.
3. In a Mortal and Pestel grind Thyme, Allspice, Nutmeg, and Salt.
4. Add Turbinado Sugar and grind into a fine, savory Sugar.
5. Pour over the Rhubarb in the metal bowl, stir to coat.
6. Add the white Sugar, and stir to coat.
7. Add crushed Rice Krispies, Corn Flakes, bread crumbs, or corn starch and flour. Stire until well-coated.
8. Pour Rhubarb into pie pan with bottom crust.
9. Dot with small pieces of butter. Cover with top crust.
9. Place pie on lowest rack in oven. Bake for 15 minutes at 450 degrees F.
10. Remove, and cover edges with foil.
11. Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C), and continue baking for 45 minutes. 

Serve warm or cold.
