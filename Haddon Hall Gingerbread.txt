HADDON HALL GINGERBREAD

I. Gingerbread
 
2 ¼  cups flour
1/3 cup sugar
1 cup dark molasses
¾ cup hot water
½  cup shortening
1 egg
1teaspoon soda
1 teaspoon ginger
1 teaspoon cinnamon
¾ teaspoon salt
 
Measure all ingredients into mixing bowl.  
Blend ½ minute on low speed.  
Beat 3 minutes medium speed. 
Pour into greased, floured 9x9x2 inch cake pan.
Bake 50 minutes in 325* oven.
Serve warm.
 
II. Old-Fashioned Lemon Sauce
 
1 cup sugar
½ cup butter
¼ water
1 egg, well beaten
¾ teaspoon grated lemon peel
3 tablespoons lemon juice
 
Combine all ingredients in medium saucepan.
Heat to boiling over medium heat, stirring constantly.
Serve warm.
 
III. Hard Sauce
 
Cream until soft ½ cup butter. 
Blend in gradually 1 ½ cups sifted confectioners’ sugar and 2 teaspoons vanilla.
 
 
Serve gingerbread warm with warm lemon sauce poured over it and Hard Sauce on top of that.
