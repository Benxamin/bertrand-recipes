STUFFING
========

Ingredients

1 Loaf, sliced White Bread
1 Loaf, sliced Whole Wheat Bread
1 stick Butter
4 Celery stalks, thinly sliced
1 White Onion, large, minced
1 Shallot, thinly sliced, then chopped
1 bunch Parsley, flat-leaf (Italian)
3 oz White Wine
2 Eggs
2 cups Chicken or Turkey Stock


Method

PREP BREAD
0. Cut the crusts of the bread 4-6 slices at a time, no heels or ends. Cut into cubes.
1. Roast bread pieces in single layer on cookie sheet at 325ºF for 15-20 minutes. Takes 2 batches.
2. Cool the bread on sheets for 1-2 hours.
3. Put croutons back into their bags and leave open so, any moisture can escape.
4. Save for up to a week.

STUFFING
1. Place bread/croutons into large metal mixing bowl.
2. In large cast iron pan or enameled dutch oven, melt 3/4 stick Butter over Med-Low heat.
3. Add Celery and Onions. Cook for 8-12 minutes until the onions are translucent.
4. Add Shallots. Cook until they are translucent, too.
5. Add Parsley. Stir until covered.
6. Add Oysters and White Wine. Cook until Oysters are cooked-through.
7. Pour entire content of the pan over the bread in the giant mixing bowl. Toss to coat.
8. Drizzle with blend of: two beaten Eggs and Chicken Stock over the herbed bread pieces. Mix well.
9. Drop the bread mixture into the pan and press into sides. 
10. Using a spatula, pry the top edges away from the sides, pressing towards center. 
11. Slice last 1/4 Butter stick into pats. Place pats in the gap along the edges and a few on top. 
12. Cover with lid or foil, and bake for 30 minutes at 350ºF. 
13. Remove covering, bake for another 10-12 minutes until bread tips are browning.
