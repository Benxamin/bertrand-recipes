BAKED CHILI LIME CHICKEN
========================

INGREDIENTS
-----------
1/4 Cup Chili Powders
1 Tablespoon Salt
1 Tablespoon Black Pepper, ground
1 Tablesppon Onion Powder
1 Tablespoon Garlic Powder
1/2 Teaspoon Cumin, ground
2 dashes Ginger, ground

1/2 Can Lime Juice, Minute-Maid, frozen (Use the other half for your margaritas)
2 Limes, juiced

2-3 lbs Chicken Thighs, eight (8)

OPTIONAL
1 Tablespoon "Chili Crunch", momofuku's chili oil


METHOD
------
1. Defrost chicken pieces in salt bath (brining).
2. Mix ingredients to make a marinade.
3. Add chicken to marinade for 3 hours.
4. Pre-heat oven to 400degF.
4. Spread marinated chicken pieces on cookie sheet lined with aluminum foil.
5. Bake in oven 33 minutes. Internal temperature should exceed 165degF.
