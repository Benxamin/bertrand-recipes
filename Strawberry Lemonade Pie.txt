STRAWBERRY LEMONADE PIE
———————————————————————

PRETZEL PIE CRUST

Ingredients

3/4 cup Butter, unsalted, softened (1.5 sticks)
3 Tbls Brown Sugar
2 1/2 cups Pretzels, crushed

Method

0. Preheat oven to 350degF.
1. Add Pretzels to food processor to crush. 
2. Mix in the Brown Sugar and Butter.
3. Press into pie pan. 
4. Bake for 10 minutes.

 * * *

STRAWBERRY LAYER

Ingredients

1 Quart Strawberries
1 cup Water
2 tablespoons Corn Starch
1 cup Sugar
1 pkg Strawberry Jell-O

Method 

0. Cut the Strawberries in half.
1. Arrange them on the bottom the pie crust.
2. Cook Water, Corn Starch and Sugar over medium heat until thick and clear. 
3. Remove from heat.
4. Whisk in 2 tablespoons strawberry Jell-O powder
5. Stir in half the Strawberries, mashed.
6. Pour over the arranged Strawberries.
7. Chill the pie crust for at least 1 hour.

 * * *

LEMON LAYER

Ingredients

1 Pkg Lemon Pudding Mix
1 Cup Milk, cold
1 tsp Lemon juice

Method

1. Mix pudding together.
2. Spread over the top of the Strawberry layer
3. Chill

 * * * 

WHIPPED CREAM LAYER

Ingredients

1. 1 Qt Heavy Whipping Cream
2. 1 Tbsp Vanilla
3. 3 Tbsp Sugar, white granulated

Method

1. Pour Heavy Whipping Cream into Stand Mixer.
2. Set on 3 with the whip attachment.
3. Pour in the Vanilla.
4. Pour in the Sugar.
5. Once incorporated, turn the speed up to 4, then 5, then 6, 7, 8.
6. When hard peaks form, you’re done!
7. Spread on pie.
