RHUBARB PIE

Ingredients
-----------
6 stalks Rhubarb, sliced
1/2 cup white granulated sugar
1/4 cup Turbinado sugar
Seven (7) Allspice berries, ground
1 tsp Cinnamon
1 tsp Salt
2 Ready-made pie crusts (in your grocerâ€™s refrigerated section)


Method
------

0. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).

1. Slice 6 big stalks of Rhubarb into a large metal bowl.

2. Dump into bowl:
	6 tbsp Flour
	1/2 cup white granulated sugar
	1/4 cup turbinado sugar
	Seven (7) Allspice berries, ground.
	1 tsp Salt

3. Stir Rhubarb in the mixture in the metal bowl until well-coated.

4. Pour Rhubarb into pie pan with bottom crust.

5. Dot with small pieces of butter. Cover with top crust.

6. Place pie on lowest rack in oven. Bake for 15 minutes at 450 degrees F.

7. Cover edges with foil

8. Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C), and continue baking for 40 to 45 minutes. 

Serve warm or cold.
