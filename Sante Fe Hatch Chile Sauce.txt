SANTA FE HATCH CHILE SAUCE

Ingredients
-----------

6 ounces Hatch chile peppers, halved and seeded
3 tablespoons corn oil
1 cup chopped onion
2 teaspoons minced garlic
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 cups beef broth
1 teaspoon salt

Method
------

0. Roast, blister, cool and peel 6 hatch chilies.
1. Heat oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
2. Add onion; cook and stir until browned, about 15 minutes. 
3. Add garlic; cook until fragrant, about 2 minutes. 
4. Stir in flour until mixture thickens. 
5. Stir in chopped chile peppers, beef broth and salt. 
6. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer until flavors combine, about 10 minutes.
