MEAT SAUCE
----------
1 pound sweet Italian sausage
Brown sausage over medium, about 12 mins.

1/2 cup minced onion.
Cook in pork sausage fat until translucent, 30 mins.

3/4 pound lean ground beef
Salt and Black Pepper to cover the beef 
4 cloves garlic, minced through press.
Brown beef over medium. Cook time is up to 60min.

 1 (28 ounce) can crushed Tomatoes 1 (12 ounce) can Tomato Paste 2 (32 ounce) can canned Tomato Sauce  
2 tablespoons white sugar 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning 3/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Simmer 1.5 hours.


RICOTTA MIXTURE
---------------
16 ounces ricotta cheese 1 egg
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
Salt and pepper

Mix well.


LASAGNA
-------
12 lasagna noodles 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese 
Preheat oven to 375degF.

Assemble by layers in a thicker pan than normal!

Bottom to top: 
- Meat sauce
- Lasagna noodles, cooked and cooled
- 1/2 Ricotta Mix.
- Mozzerella cheese
- Meat sauce + grated Parmesan cheese
- Lasagna noodles, cooked and cooled
- 1/2 Ricotta Mix.
- Mozzerella cheese
- Meat sauce + grated Parmesan cheese
- Lasagna noodles
- Last of the sauce
- Last of the shredded Mozzarella.

Bake covered with foil for 25-30 minutes.
Remove foil, bake for 25 minutes.
Cool at least 15 minutes before serving.
