PULLED PORK HATCH CHILE STEW


Ingredients
-----------
2 tablespoons canola oil
1 yellow onion, diced
1 cup diced carrot
2 Hatch chile peppers, peeled and seeded
3 cloves garlic, diced
salt and ground black pepper to taste

1 1/2 pounds shredded cooked pork
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
4 cups chicken broth
1 (15 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
2 cups cubed potatoes
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin


Method
------
0. Heat oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. 
1. Add onion and carrot; cook and stir until almost soft, about 15 minutes. 2. Add chile peppers and garlic; cook and stir until fragrant, about 2 minutes. 
3. Stir in pork, season with salt and pepper.
4. Stir in all-purpose flour.
5. Stir chicken broth slowly into the pot. 
6. Add tomatoes, potatoes, and cumin. 
7. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and cover. 
8. Simmer, stirring occasionally, until potatoes are tender, 20 to 25 minutes.
