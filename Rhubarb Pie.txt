RHUBARB PIE

Ingredients
-----------
6 stalks Rhubarb, sliced
1 Lime, juiced
1/4 tsp Allspice berries, or 1/8 tsp Allspice ground.
1 Clove
1/8 tsp Nutmeg, ground
4 Green Peppercorns
4 White Perrercorns
1/2 tsp Salt
1 1/2 cup Sugar, white granulated
1 tsp Corn Starch
2 Tbsp Flour, heaping
2 Tbsp Butter
2 Ready-made pie crusts (in your grocerâ€™s refrigerated section)


Method
------

0. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
1. Slice 6 big stalks of Rhubarb into a large metal bowl.
2. Squeeze in the juice of one Lime.
3. In a mortar and pestel grind Allspice berries, Clove and Peppercorns with the Nutmeg and Salt.
4. Take the powdery seasoning and sprinkle it over the Rhubarb.
5. Add Sugar, Corns Starch and Flour. Stir the Rhubarb pieces until well coated, and syrup is uniform.
6. Pour Rhubarb into pie pan with bottom crust.
7. Dot with small pieces of butter.
8. Cover with top crust.
9. Place pie on lowest rack in oven. Bake for 15 minutes at 450 degrees F.
10. Remove, and cover edges with foil.
11. Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C), and continue baking for 45 minutes. 

Serve warm or cold. Rhubarb is so sour, you need the sugar. I'm thinking of trying this with kirsh or rum next...
